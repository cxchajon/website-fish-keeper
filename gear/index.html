Quick Answers

What size heater do I need?
Use 5 watts per gallon as a baseline. A 20-gallon tank needs ~100W.
How much filtration is enough?
Aim for 5-7x tank volume per hour (GPH). A 40-gallon needs 200-280 GPH.
Do I need an air pump?
If your filter agitates the surface, you have enough oxygen. But a battery-backed air pump is smart insurance for power outages.
What light spectrum grows plants?
Full-spectrum LED in the 6,500K-7,500K range. For easy plants, aim for PAR 30-50 at substrate.
