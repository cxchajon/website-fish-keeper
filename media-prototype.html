Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Tank Guide?

The Tank Guide is the FishKeepingLifeCo learning platform dedicated to freshwater beginners. We publish clear videos, hands-on guides, and illustrated books that make aquarium science approachable for families, classrooms, and first-time hobbyists.

What is “Life in Balance” about?

“Life in Balance” is our family-friendly storybook that turns the nitrogen cycle, filtration, and fish care basics into an illustrated adventure. It helps new aquarists understand the science behind stable tanks while keeping the narrative uplifting and easy to follow.

Where can I find nitrogen cycle guides?

Start with the Aquarium University lessons, then bookmark the quick-reference sheets in the Aquarium Library. You can also watch our YouTube tutorials for step-by-step nitrogen cycle walkthroughs.

Do you feature community tanks?

Absolutely. We showcase reader aquariums in the Featured Tanks gallery, spotlight a Community Video Pick, and invite hobbyists to share builds through the Feature Your Tank form.
