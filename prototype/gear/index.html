Water Quality & Maintenance Essentials

Testing Kits & Monitoring

Keep ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, and pH on track with a liquid master test kit and weekly log. Pair results with the Stocking Advisor to validate your bioload and chart stability with weekly entries synced to your University lessons.

  • Liquid master test kit (API or equivalent)
  • Digital thermometer for daily spot-checks
  • Inline TDS pen for planted tanks

Cleanup & Extras Toolkit

Round out maintenance days with supportive gear that keeps mess to a minimum and electronics safe from moisture.

  • Microfiber towels: Keep a pack of lint-free cloths handy so glass stays streak-free between deep cleans.
  • Nitrile gloves: Dedicate aquarium-only gloves to protect your skin and prevent lotions from entering the water.
  • Dedicated 5-gallon bucket: Label one bucket strictly for aquarium use to avoid soap residue when you move water.
  • Silicone mat or tray: Stage wet tools on a waterproof mat so outlets and controllers stay dry.

Stocking & Compatibility Tips

Matching Gear to Bioload

Balance filtration, heating, and aeration with your planned fish community. Start with the Stocking Advisor to model your bioload, then reference the Cycling Coach to confirm flow and heater sizing before adding livestock.

Preventing Stress & Disease

Keep a small hospital tote with sponge filter, heater, and conditioned water ready for quarantines. Follow the filter media cleaning tutorial to avoid washing away bacteria, and review our betta community setup tips before mixing species with different temperaments.
