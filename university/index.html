Aquarium University

Structured learning that feels like parchment-lined halls opening into a calm, coastal lab. Blend academic rigor with the serenity of aquatic exploration.

Guided Study Tracks

Choose from hybrid paths that weave foundational aquatic biology with practical aquarium workflows. Each track pairs readings, lab demonstrations, and reflective assessments to reinforce mastery.

  • Freshwater Foundations: Water chemistry, bioload balancing, and core ecology principles.
  • Habitat Design Studios: Aquascaping critiques, planting labs, and filtration strategy workshops.
  • Care & Welfare Clinics: Preventative health, nutritional planning, and enrichment case studies.

Immersive Lab Sessions

Hands-on practicums provide tactile reinforcement of lecture concepts. Labs emphasize sensory cues — color, flow, microfauna activity — to connect theory with the living systems you steward.

Enrollments include downloadable lab briefs, video walkthroughs, and data sheets that make documentation effortless.

Certificates & Continuing Resources

Earn digital certificates that verify competencies for community clubs or retail partnerships. Alumni gain access to quarterly masterclasses, research roundups, and equipment benchmarks curated by The Tank Guide.

Planning to teach others? Request our facilitator toolkit to bring Aquarium University modules to your local makerspace or aquarium society.
