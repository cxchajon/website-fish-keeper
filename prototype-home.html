Aquarium Science, Simplified for Everyone

Welcome to The Tank Guide, where we make the science of aquariums simple, beautiful, and accessible for everyone. We know that starting your first fish tank can feel like a deep dive into a world of complex chemistry and conflicting advice. Our mission, powered by FishKeepingLifeCo, is to be the clear, calm voice that guides you from confusion to confidence.

Master the Foundation: Understanding the Nitrogen Cycle

At the heart of every stable aquarium is one crucial process: the nitrogen cycle. It’s the invisible engine that turns fish waste into harmless nitrates, creating a safe environment for your aquatic life. Instead of just telling you about it, we’ve built the Cycling Coach—an interactive tool that lets you track your tank’s progress and understand each stage.

Build a Balanced Community with Our Stocking Advisor

Once your tank is cycled and ready, the real fun begins: choosing your fish. Our Stocking Advisor helps you plan peaceful, sustainable aquariums.

Learn with Us: From Our Tank to Yours

Everything we teach is backed by real-world experience. Our guides and articles are directly informed by our own ongoing research and testing, where we document everything from fertilizer dosing to fish behavior. Follow along in the Media Hub. This is science in action, not just in theory.

Beginner FAQs

What is the most important thing to know before starting a fish tank?

The most important concept is the nitrogen cycle… Our Cycling Coach tool is designed to help you track this process.

How many fish can I put in my aquarium?

It depends on species’ adult size, waste production (bioload), and your filtration capacity… Use our free Stocking Advisor.

What basic equipment do I need for a freshwater aquarium?

Tank, filter sized for volume, heater, thermometer, substrate, water conditioner, and lighting… See our Gear Guide.
