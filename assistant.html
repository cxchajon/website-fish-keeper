See it in action

Here's how the assistant helps with common aquarium questions:

Example: "Can I add 6 neon tetras to my 20 gallon?"

Assistant: "Yes! Neon tetras are perfect for a 20 gallon. They're peaceful, low bioload, and look stunning in groups. Six is a good school size.

For a detailed bioload check and to see what else you could add, try the Stocking Advisor at thetankguide.com/stocking-advisor.html—it'll calculate your exact capacity based on your filter type and current stock."

Example: "My tank is cloudy white—is this bad?"

Assistant: "That sounds like a bacterial bloom, which is actually harmless—just looks scary. It happens when beneficial bacteria multiply rapidly, often in new tanks or after big water changes.

Here's what to do: Keep your filter running, don't do extra water changes (that can restart it), and it should clear in 3-7 days on its own. If your fish are acting stressed or you see ammonia/nitrite spikes, that's a different issue—test your water and let me know the numbers."

Example: "Should I replace my filter cartridge monthly?"

Assistant: "No—that's the 'filter cartridge conspiracy.' Beneficial bacteria colonize your filter media, so replacing it crashes your biological cycle.

Instead: Rinse your media in old tank water during water changes (not tap water—chlorine kills bacteria). Only replace it when it's physically falling apart. Use ceramic rings or sponge for long-term media that you never replace."

FAQ

Is this free?

Yes. We created The Tank Guide Assistant to give beginners a safe, no-cost starting point. Standard ChatGPT access is enough.

Do I need ChatGPT Plus?

No. The assistant works with free ChatGPT accounts. If you already have Plus, it will work there too.

Is this a veterinarian?

No. The assistant is educational only and does not replace professional veterinary care. Always consult a qualified vet for animal health decisions.

Can it read water tests?

It cannot read images. Type in your test numbers, and it will help you interpret ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, and pH ranges.

Does it replace research?

No. Use the assistant to guide your learning, then cross-check with trusted sources like The Tank Guide, manufacturer manuals, and experienced hobbyists.

How does it know about The Tank Guide's tools and content?

The assistant is trained on The Tank Guide's complete knowledge base—including how the Stocking Advisor calculates bioload, what the Cycling Coach tracks, gear testing results, nitrogen cycle explanations, and real tank maintenance logs. It references these resources and directs you to specific tools when calculations or tracking are needed.

What if it gives me different advice than the website?

The assistant follows the same principles and safety guidelines as The Tank Guide. If you notice conflicting information, trust the website tools for calculations (Stocking Advisor, Cycling Coach) and use the assistant for explanations and planning help. You can also check the specific blog or guide it references.

Does it remember my previous conversations?

Yes, within the same chat session. The assistant remembers context from earlier in your conversation, so you can ask follow-up questions. However, it doesn't retain information between separate chat sessions—you'll need to re-share your tank specs if you start a new conversation.

Can it help me choose between products?

Yes. The assistant knows which gear The Tank Guide has tested and recommended, including Seachem products (Stability, Prime, Purigen, Excel), filter brands (Fluval, AquaClear, Seachem Tidal), and when to prioritize budget vs. premium options. It can explain the practical differences based on real-world use.
