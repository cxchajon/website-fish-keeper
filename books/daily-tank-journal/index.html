The Daily Tank Journal

A Structured Foundation for Consistent Aquarium Care and Fish Welfare

A healthy aquarium isn't the result of luck or expensive gear—it is the result of observation and consistent routines.

The Daily Tank Journal provides hobbyists with a grounded, paper-based system to document the life of their aquarium. By moving away from guesswork and toward data-driven care, you can better understand the unique patterns of your ecosystem and ensure the long-term welfare of your fish.

Developed by FishKeepingLifeCo, this journal is a companion for those who value patience and the science of the nitrogen cycle over quick fixes.

The Purpose of Documentation

In fishkeeping, stability is the ultimate goal. Keeping a dedicated log helps you:

  • Protect Fish Welfare — Identify subtle changes in behavior or water chemistry before they become emergencies.
  • Master the Nitrogen Cycle — Track the transition from ammonia to nitrate to ensure a safe environment for your livestock.
  • Build Reliable Routines — Standardize water changes and filter care to prevent inconsistent maintenance.
  • Simplify Troubleshooting — Maintain a historical record of changes for faster problem diagnosis.

What's Included in Your Log

This 214-page, six-month journal is designed for clarity and ease of use, featuring:

  • Daily Observation Logs — Feedings, behavior patterns, and general tank health
  • Water Chemistry Tracking — pH, Ammonia (NH₃/NH₄⁺), Nitrite (NO₂⁻), Nitrate (NO₃⁻), Temperature
  • Weekly Care Summaries — Water change volumes and routine upkeep
  • Monthly Reflection Pages — Trend review and long-term stability tracking
  • Livestock & Reference Sheets — Species lists and emergency notes for tank-sitters

Is This Journal Right for Your Tank?

This edition is designed for standard freshwater setups, including:

  • Betta fish habitats
  • Community aquariums (tetras, rasboras, livebearers)
  • Species-specific tanks (goldfish, cichlids, invertebrates)
