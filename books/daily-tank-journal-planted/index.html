The Daily Tank Journal: Planted Edition

Advanced Planted Tank Documentation for CO₂, Fertilization, and Plant Growth

Managing a planted aquarium requires precision beyond basic water chemistry. Plant health, CO₂ injection, fertilizer dosing schedules, and PAR measurements create a complex ecosystem that demands careful observation and consistent tracking.

The Daily Tank Journal: Planted Edition provides dedicated fields for aquascapers and planted tank enthusiasts to document both core water parameters and plant-specific metrics. By maintaining detailed records, you can fine-tune your dosing regimens, track plant growth patterns, and achieve the stable, lush aquascape you envision.

Developed by FishKeepingLifeCo, this journal is designed for those managing intensive planted systems who need structured documentation to support long-term success.

Why Track Plant-Specific Parameters

Planted tanks introduce additional variables that affect both plant health and livestock welfare. Detailed logging helps you:

  • Optimize CO₂ Systems — Track injection rates, bubble counts, and drop checker readings to maintain safe, effective levels.
  • Master Fertilizer Dosing — Document macro and micronutrient schedules to prevent deficiencies and algae blooms.
  • Monitor Plant Growth — Record trimming schedules, propagation notes, and visual health indicators.
  • Balance Light & Nutrients — Track PAR values and correlate lighting duration with nutrient uptake.
  • Maintain Water Chemistry — Continue tracking pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, and temperature alongside plant metrics.

What's Included in This Planted Edition

This comprehensive journal extends the core Daily Tank Journal structure with plant-specific tracking fields:

  • Daily Plant Observations — Growth rates, color changes, new leaf development, and visible deficiencies
  • CO₂ Injection Tracking — Bubble count, drop checker color, injection duration, and adjustments
  • Fertilizer Dosing Logs — Macro dosing (NPK), micro dosing (trace elements), and custom supplement schedules
  • Lighting & PAR Documentation — Photoperiod length, PAR measurements at substrate level, fixture adjustments
  • Core Water Chemistry — pH, Ammonia (NH₃/NH₄⁺), Nitrite (NO₂⁻), Nitrate (NO₃⁻), GH, KH, Temperature
  • Weekly Maintenance Summaries — Water change volumes, trimming sessions, substrate vacuuming
  • Monthly Aquascaping Review — Layout evolution, plant species performance, long-term trend analysis
  • Plant & Livestock Reference Sheets — Species lists, placement maps, care requirements

Is the Planted Edition Right for Your Tank?

This edition is designed for intensive planted aquariums, including:

  • High-tech planted tanks with CO₂ injection
  • Dutch-style aquascapes with demanding plant species
  • Nature aquarium (Iwagumi, Ryoboku) with precision dosing
  • Competition aquascapes requiring detailed growth documentation

For standard freshwater setups without intensive plant dosing, check out the Daily Tank Journal (standard edition).

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the Planted Edition different from the standard Daily Tank Journal?

The Planted Edition includes dedicated tracking fields for CO₂ injection rates, fertilizer dosing schedules (macros and micros), PAR measurements, plant growth observations, and aquascaping maintenance. It's designed for high-tech planted tanks that require precision documentation beyond basic water chemistry.

Do I need a CO₂ system to use this journal?

While this journal is optimized for high-tech planted tanks with CO₂ injection, it can also be valuable for low-tech setups with demanding plant species. You can use the sections relevant to your setup and skip fields that don't apply (like CO₂ tracking if you're running a low-tech tank).

Can I track both plants and fish welfare?

Yes. The Planted Edition includes all core water chemistry parameters (pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, temperature) and livestock observation fields from the standard edition, plus expanded plant-specific sections. This ensures you can monitor both plant health and fish welfare in one journal.

What fertilizer dosing methods does this journal support?

The journal provides flexible dosing logs that work with EI (Estimative Index), PPS-Pro, lean dosing methods, or custom regimens. You can document both liquid and dry fertilizers, track macro/micro schedules separately, and note any adjustments based on plant response.

Is this journal suitable for beginners?

This journal is designed for planted tank enthusiasts who are already familiar with CO₂ systems, fertilizer dosing, and plant care fundamentals. If you're new to planted tanks, consider starting with the standard Daily Tank Journal and upgrading to the Planted Edition once you introduce CO₂ or more demanding plant species.

How long does this journal last?

Like the standard edition, this journal is designed for six months of daily logging. The extended tracking fields for plants and dosing schedules make it a comprehensive record of your aquascape's evolution over that period.
