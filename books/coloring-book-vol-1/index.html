Exploring the Freshwater Ecosystem Through Art

This volume focuses on the visual characteristics and biological needs of popular freshwater fish. By featuring specific betta varieties and community species, it helps beginners recognize the diversity of the hobby while building a foundation for responsible fishkeeping.

The book extends FishKeepingLifeCo's mission: making fishkeeping less intimidating and more humane by teaching the reasoning behind proper care. These illustrations offer a grounded, accessible way to become familiar with the fish that might live in your tank.
