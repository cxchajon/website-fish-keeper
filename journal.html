Tank Reaches 100% BBA-Free Status

December 5, 2025

After two months of Excel spot treatments and strategic flow adjustments, the last visible BBA tuft disappeared from the Anubias leaves. The combination of consistent CO2, improved circulation from the new sponge placement, and weekly Excel maintenance has completely eliminated the algae without any plant damage.

Key Success Factors:

  • Daily Excel spot dosing on problem areas
  • Amano shrimp grazing on weakened BBA
  • Improved flow after removing poly-fil
  • Stable nitrates (5-10 ppm range)

This is a major milestone for tank health and aesthetics. All plants showing strong, clean growth.
