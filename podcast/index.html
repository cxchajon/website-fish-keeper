About the podcast

The freshwater aquarium podcast where science meets stories.

We believe fishkeeping is more than maintenance—it's a pursuit of balance. Through candid conversations with local fish store owners, deep dives into the nitrogen cycle, gear reviews, and real-time tank journals, we explore the beautiful complexity of this hobby.

Hosted by FishKeepingLifeCo and powered by TheTankGuide.com, this is your audio companion for unpacking the science behind the tank and sharing honest lessons from the journey.
