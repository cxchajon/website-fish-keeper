Cycle Check: The 3-Reading Rule

Before you stock, lock this in: consistent readings prevent stress down the road.

The rule: Get three consecutive days of 0 ppm ammonia, 0 ppm nitrite, and stable nitrate (not dropping to 0).

If there’s a blip: pause and recheck in 48–72 hours. Patience here saves fish later.

Pro tip: Take photos of your test kit next to a dated note — easy proof and pattern tracking.
