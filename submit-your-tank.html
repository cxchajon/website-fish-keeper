    Submit Your Tank — FAQ

    Quick answers about the new 6-step submission, duplicate protection, and editing credits.

    How does the submission flow work?

    Upload at least 3 photos (optionally add 2 more for $1), then choose to self-write your story or have FKLC craft it for you with the $1 editing package.

    Optional upgrades include extra photos, editing credits, and additional tanks with editing included.

    What's included in the $1 editing package?
    • Professional first draft (does not use a credit).
    • 3 revision rounds that never expire and stay tied to the tank.
    • Credits can be used later for updates to the same feature.
    How are duplicates prevented?

    We cross-check email + tank name similarity and tank size. If we spot a potential match (>80% similarity), you'll see a warning before payment and we'll flag it for admin review.

    How is pricing calculated?
    • First tank: free when you self-write + 3 photos.
    • Editing package: +$1 (first tank only).
    • Extra photos: +$1 for 2 extra slots per tank.
    • Each additional tank: $2 (includes editing + 3 revisions) with optional +$1 extra photos per additional tank.
    When will I hear back?

    Expect the first draft or review note within 5–7 days. Credits remain available for future tweaks even after publication.

    Do I keep ownership of my photos?

    You keep ownership. You grant us a non-exclusive license so we can host, credit, and promote your feature across The Tank Guide channels.
