Store FAQs

Who are these books for?

Beginners setting up their first tank, families learning together, and hobbyists who want a simple, guided way to track aquarium care without designing their own layout. Everyone from kids to adults will find something useful here.

What’s the difference between the Daily Tank Journal and the Planted Edition?

Both help you track water parameters and daily care. The Planted Edition adds dedicated sections for CO₂ dosing, lighting schedules, nutrient tracking, and plant growth—perfect for anyone keeping or planning to keep live aquatic plants.

Do I need both journals or just one?

Most people choose the one that matches their setup. If you have a fish-only freshwater tank, the standard journal is perfect. If you’re adding plants soon, the Planted Edition gives you room for CO₂, lighting, and nutrient notes. If you’re staying unplanted for now but might try plants later, start with the regular journal—you can always upgrade to the Planted Edition when you’re ready.

Are the books beginner-friendly?

Absolutely. Everything is written in simple, clear language. Even the storybook Life in Balance teaches aquarium fundamentals in a way that kids, parents, and brand-new hobbyists can all understand and enjoy.

Do the coloring books actually teach fishkeeping?

Yes! Every page includes real aquarium concepts—species information, ecosystem balance, and care basics. They’re designed to be both fun and genuinely educational for all ages.

Is there a recommended order to use these books?

Not at all—each book stands alone. That said, many people start with Life in Balance to learn the core concepts, then pick up a journal or coloring book based on what fits their interests or tank type.
