Aquarium Science, Simplified for Everyone

Welcome to The Tank Guide, where we make the science of aquariums simple, beautiful, and accessible for everyone. We know that starting your first fish tank can feel like a deep dive into a world of complex chemistry and conflicting advice. Our mission, powered by FishKeepingLifeCo, is to be the clear, calm voice that guides you from confusion to confidence.

Master the Foundation: Understanding the Nitrogen Cycle

At the heart of every stable aquarium is one crucial process: the nitrogen cycle. It’s the invisible engine that turns fish waste into harmless nitrates, creating a safe environment for your aquatic life. Instead of just telling you about it, we’ve built the Cycling Coach—an interactive tool that lets you track your tank’s progress and understand each stage. This isn’t just data; it’s the foundation of responsible fishkeeping, and we’re here to walk you through every step.

Build a Balanced Community with Our Stocking Advisor

Once your tank is cycled and ready, the real fun begins: choosing your fish. But how many can you add? Will they get along? A healthy tank is a balanced ecosystem, not just a collection of pretty fish. That’s why we developed our Stocking Advisor. It goes beyond simple ‘one inch of fish per gallon’ rules, using logic based on bioload, behavior, and filtration capacity. This tool, born from our own research and testing, helps you build a peaceful and thriving underwater community without the stress of guesswork. Use it to test your ideas in real time and plan a tank that’s both beautiful and sustainable.

Beginner Aquarium FAQ

What is The Tank Guide?

The Tank Guide is a free educational resource from FishKeepingLifeCo. We provide easy-to-understand guides, a fish tank stocking calculator, a nitrogen cycle tracker, and gear recommendations to help beginners successfully set up a freshwater aquarium.

How many fish can I put in my aquarium?

It depends on species’ adult size, behavior, waste production (bioload), and your filtration capacity. Use our free Stocking Advisor to plan a safe, balanced community for your tank.

What is the aquarium nitrogen cycle?

The nitrogen cycle is a natural 4–8 week process where beneficial bacteria convert toxic ammonia into safer nitrate. Complete cycling before adding fish—our Cycling Coach guides you step-by-step.
